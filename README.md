Clipo is a free, modern clipboard manager for macOS，designed to make copying, pasting, organizing, and transferring content faster and easier. It keeps your clipboard history ready at any time, allowing you to quickly recall text, images, links, files, and other copied content through a beautiful liquid-glass interface. Whether you prefer keyboard shortcuts, panel selection, double-click paste, drag-and-drop, or quick actions, Clipo provides multiple flexible ways to paste and reuse your clipboard items with minimal interruption to your workflow.
Beyond simple clipboard history, Clipo also works as a lightweight file staging station. You can temporarily collect files, quickly drag them into other apps, or send them via AirDrop directly from the panel. With built-in AI extension support, Clipo can help you search through clipboard history, understand copied content, summarize text, process selected clipboard items, and make your clipboard more intelligent and useful.
Built with a clean SwiftUI interface and a modern liquid-glass visual style, Clipo is lightweight, elegant, and productivity-focused. It is made for users who copy, paste, move files, and switch between apps every day - helping turn the clipboard into a faster, smarter, and more convenient workspace.
